Progression Guide: First Run to 100%

This guide is meant to feel like a real playthrough — not a speedrun route, not a low-spoiler mystery route, not a spreadsheet. The goal is to help you move from the opening pools to the endgame while quietly setting up a full-completion file instead of reaching the final stretch with half the game still unfinished.

It is written to stay spoiler-light on hidden content and late secrets, but practical about what actually blocks progress. When a mission wall has a specific requirement, this guide names it and tells you where to find it — only at the moment it becomes relevant, not front-loaded.

25 Main Pools
34 Missions
17 Crafting Recipes
9 Stages Covered

How to Use This Guide

  • Treat each stage like a phase of the run, not a rigid checklist.
  • Push forward when your current pool is still giving XP, money, and bestiary progress at the same time.
  • Rotate away when a branch starts asking for one very specific thing and everything else has slowed down.
  • Finish pools that are already close to 100% instead of carrying lots of nearly-finished work into the postgame.
  • Keep at least one copy of unusual fish whenever possible until you are sure they are not tied to missions, hunts, crafting, or appraise targets.
  • When you see a Key Bottlenecks block in a stage, read it when you hit a wall — not necessarily before.

What "100%" Means Here

  • All 25 main bestiary pools at 100%
  • All 34 missions completed
  • All hunts cleared at least once
  • All 17 crafting recipes completed
  • All rods that count toward rod completion collected
  • Global fish, rod, and pool bestiary rewards claimed

The repo has 27 pools total, but 2 hidden pools are excluded from main bestiary completion. Some tribute or secret rods are also excluded from rod bestiary completion. This guide treats hidden late-game content as bonus completion after the core route is already in good shape.

The Four-Part Rhythm of a Healthy Run

1
Mission progress that unlocks a new pool, rod, or branch.
2
Bestiary progress in pools that are currently relevant.
3
Side power from rods, crafting, upgrades, appraise, or mutation projects.
4
Cleanup in older zones that are already almost solved.

If you keep those four moving together, the game feels broad and rewarding. Think of the four rhythms as a pulse — each session, at least two should move. Three is better. All four at once means you are playing very efficiently.

What Actually Drives Progression

Progression is not only about levels.

  • Levels — gate several major missions, including some that cannot be rushed regardless of how much money you have.
  • Bestiary percent — many pools open from discovery, not money alone.
  • Mutations — some branches ask for them directly, and the rarest ones require intentional setup, not just luck.
  • Rod comfort — the minigame becomes a real skill check in midgame and beyond. A rod you trust beats a rod with better stats that you fumble.
  • Crafting — some rods are solutions to later problems, and some of the best cannot be bought at all.
  • Money — matters more than it looks early on. Several pool unlocks have costs that are easy to overlook.

General Principles That Save Time

Value control early

In the early and midgame, control often matters more than headline luck. A rod that feels stable produces better XP, cleaner catches, and less frustration. Upgrading control on a rod you already trust is often a better investment than chasing a new one.

Do not appraise random filler

Appraise matters most when the fish already has a reason to matter:

  • it is part of a mission wall
  • it is a useful mutation target
  • it is tied to a craft
  • it is a valuable rare catch worth refining

Upgrade rods you actually use

  • upgrade control when catches are getting tense
  • upgrade luck when a rod is carrying a wide slice of progression
  • upgrade kg_max when you are losing fish that would otherwise matter

Do not sell everything

Missions, crafting, hunts, and upgrades all reward the player who keeps inventory depth. Get into the habit of holding one extra copy of anything rare or unusual.

Watch your money alongside your level

Grande Recife, Geleira Snowcap, Baia do Sol, and the Marianas Prologue all require spending real money in addition to meeting level or bestiary thresholds.

Bestiary cleanup is strongest done in waves

A pool at 92% is worth much more attention than a pool at 18%. Finish clusters of nearly-complete pools along the way, not at the very end.

Stage 1 Settle In
Roughly levels 1–4

Main goals

  • Finish Primeira Pesca.
  • Finish Vendedor Iniciante.
  • Reach 60% of Lagoa Tranquila.
  • Start building a small money cushion.

What to do

Spend enough time in Lagoa Tranquila to become comfortable with the minigame. The best early habit is not "catch fast," but "catch clean."

Mar Aberto is already available — do not treat it as later content. It is your first natural money break and your first reminder that rotating pools is healthy.

Primeira Pesca only needs 3 catches. Complete it immediately. It unlocks Vendedor Iniciante, which only needs 3 sold fish. Complete that too, right away — it is what actually starts the path toward Pantano Mushgrove, Baia do Sol, and the Vara Brilhante unlock.

Key Bottlenecks
Olho no Bestiário: 60% of Lagoa Tranquila. No specific fish — just discovery.
Vendedor Iniciante: Sell 3 fish. This triggers the Pantano Mushgrove and Baia do Sol chains — do not delay it.

Suggestions

  • If the early rods all feel weak, Vara de Carbono is the safest all-around pickup.
  • Start keeping Sardinha from Mar Aberto — you will deliver 50 of them after unlocking Geleira Snowcap.
  • If Lagoa Tranquila stalls on one late fish, move on and come back later.
Stage 2 River, Swamp, and First Branching
Roughly levels 4–7

Main goals

  • Unlock Rio Correnteza and push it to 80%.
  • Unlock Pantano Mushgrove.
  • Reach level 5 and spend 400 for Grande Recife.
  • Reach level 5 and spend 1200 for Geleira Snowcap.
  • Reach level 7 and spend 1500 for Baia do Sol.

What to do

Rio Correnteza is your first real lesson in bestiary-based progression. You want 80% there before Pantano Mushgrove opens.

Do not read the unlock costs as "pick one." Open all three when possible, then rotate. Geleira Snowcap unlocks the penguin side missions and the Celestia unlock chain, so it is not just a detour. Baia do Sol also later launches the Fossa das Marianas chain.

Key Bottlenecks
Unlock Pantano Mushgrove: 80% of Rio Correnteza.
Unlock Grande Recife: Level 5 and 400 money.
Unlock Geleira Snowcap: Level 5 and 1200 money. Also starts the Celestia and penguin mission chains.
Unlock Baia do Sol: Level 7 and 1500 money.
If you have Sardinha stocked up, the first penguin mission becomes available after Snowcap is unlocked. Delivering 50 is a low-effort early reward.
Stage 3 Reef Progress and the Garden Pivot
Roughly levels 5–9

Main goals

  • Build serious progress in Grande Recife.
  • Push Pantano Mushgrove to 50%.
  • Unlock O Jardim.
  • Start the Densa Mata chain.
  • Begin treating crafting as part of progression, not optional extra content.

What to do

Grande Recife is one of the most important midgame pools — 80% plus one key catch opens Profundezas Desoladas. Open O Jardim as soon as you can (50% of Pantano Mushgrove and 1000 money), then split sessions between:

  • Grande Recife for main-route momentum
  • Pantano Mushgrove for swamp progression
  • O Jardim for long-term side progression and crafts

When both Pantano Mushgrove and O Jardim reach 50%, Densa Mata: Prologo opens. Start watching for Envenenado catches in the swamp now — easier to accumulate passively than to farm in one go.

Key Bottlenecks
Unlock O Jardim: 50% of Pantano Mushgrove and 1000 money.
Densa Mata: Prologo: 50% of Pantano Mushgrove and 50% of O Jardim. Both need to move together.
Densa Mata Part 1 (preview): 4 fish with Envenenado and 1 Vieira-Lama carrying Envenenado — both from Pantano Mushgrove.
Profundezas Desoladas (preview): 80% of Grande Recife plus 1 Imperador do Coral. Hold every copy you catch.

Rod and craft suggestions

Start building materials for Trinity, Perforatio, Frenesis, Midas, and Magnasas — but do not let crafting become your only goal.

Stage 4 Desert and Sun-Coast Setup
Roughly levels 9–12

Main goals

  • Reach level 9 and unlock Deserto Taara.
  • Open A Fonte.
  • Push Baia do Sol toward Vulcao do Sol.
  • Keep Pantano Mushgrove and O Jardim active enough that Densa Mata does not fall behind.

What to do

Deserto Taara opens at level 9, but only after delivering 1 fish carrying the Arenoso mutation. Pay attention to any Arenoso catches before you hit level 9.

The full chain toward Vulcao do Sol and then Fossa das Marianas is longer than it looks — the prologue requires a fish that only exists in Vulcao do Sol. Do not let the sun-coast branch sit at "I will do Vulcao do Sol later."

Key Bottlenecks
Unlock Deserto Taara: Level 9 and 1 fish with Arenoso mutation delivered.
Unlock A Fonte: 70% of Deserto Taara and 2 delivered Arenoso fish.
Calor que Forja (Ouro Fervente rod): 1 Xeique de Taara, 1 Serpente Dunária with Incinerado, and 5000 money earned total.
Unlock Vulcao do Sol: 75% of Baia do Sol, level 12, and 5 fish carrying the Coral mutation.
Fossa das Marianas Prologue (critical dependency): 2000 money and 1 Banshee Ígnea — a Legendary from Vulcao do Sol. You cannot begin the Marianas chain until you have caught one.
Stage 5 Vertigo, Deep Reef, and the Midgame Wall
Roughly levels 10–15

Main goals

  • Push Mar Aberto to 80% and level 10 to unlock Vertigo.
  • Push Grande Recife to 80% and catch Imperador do Coral to unlock Profundezas Desoladas.
  • Pick up Vertigem if it matches your playstyle.
  • Use hunts and side routes to break up the grind.

What to do

You are advancing two major routes in parallel. Profundezas Desoladas fans out into multiple unlocks:

  • 60% opens Piscina de Salmoura
  • 70% plus a bright river fish opens Caverna Luminosa
  • A separate mission chain unlocks Tridente

As profundezas requires level 18, 2 Barracunfunda with Brilhante, and 1 Artefato-Espiral — all from Vertigo. Start banking now.

Key Bottlenecks
Unlock Vertigo: 80% of Mar Aberto and level 10.
Vertigem rod: 2 Polvo Luminoso and 1 Pato-Borracha (all from Vertigo).
Unlock Profundezas Desoladas: 80% of Grande Recife and 1 Imperador do Coral.
Unlock Caverna Luminosa: 70% of Profundezas Desoladas and 1 Surubim with Brilhante (from Rio Correnteza).
Tridente rod: 1 Madeira Lunar (Geleira Snowcap), 2 Fantasma de Salmoura (Piscina de Salmoura), and 1 fish with Elétrico mutation.
As profundezas (bank now): Level 18, 2 Barracunfunda with Brilhante, 1 Artefato-Espiral. All from Vertigo. Needs Vara Brilhante equipped.
Buy Vara Brilhante before a bright-fish requirement becomes urgent — it is available in the market once unlocked via Vendedor Iniciante.
Stage 6 Caves, Sky, and Late-Midgame Breadth
Roughly levels 14–18

Main goals

  • Push Profundezas Desoladas to 70%.
  • Unlock Piscina de Salmoura and Caverna Luminosa.
  • Unlock Caverna Carmesim.
  • Reach level 15 and unlock Celestia.
  • Reach level 18 and unlock As profundezas.

What to do

This is the broadest stage in the game. Stop trying to finish one route before touching the others. Caverna Carmesim requires a specific hunt catch with a specific mutation — the hunt runs in Caverna Luminosa, so you need to be there when it fires.

Celestia opens at level 15 with fish that come from Caverna Luminosa — bank them before level 15 arrives so the unlock is seamless.

Key Bottlenecks
Unlock Caverna Luminosa: 70% of Profundezas Desoladas and 1 Surubim carrying Brilhante.
Unlock Caverna Carmesim: 70% of Caverna Luminosa and 1 Dragão Azul with Carmesim. The Dragão Azul is the boss of the O Chamado Azul hunt — do not sell it.
Unlock Celestia: Level 15, 2 Carpa Eterea, and 2 Peixe Farol (both from Caverna Luminosa).
Unlock As profundezas: Level 18, 2 Barracunfunda with Brilhante, and 1 Artefato-Espiral (all from Vertigo).
Buy Promessa Luminescente when Caverna Luminosa opens if you plan to stay on the cave path — it makes the Prometido mutation much easier to produce.
Stage 7 Mariana Staircase
Late game into early endgame

Main goals

  • Open Rachadura Vulcanica through the Marianas prologue.
  • Advance through Zona Glacial, Zenite Abissal, and Aguas Calmas.
  • Complete the sub-zone 100% requirements that unlock Ponto Zero.

What to do

The Marianas route is a staircase — each step requires fish from the pool you just finished. This branch contains mandatory 100% progression. Fossa das Marianas: O Fundo requires 100% of Rachadura Vulcanica, Zona Glacial, Zenite Abissal, and Aguas Calmas, plus 12000 money spent. Finish these sub-zones while they are tied to forward motion — not as postgame cleanup.

Key Bottlenecks
Marianas Prologue: 2000 money and 1 Banshee Ígnea (Legendary from Vulcao do Sol).
Parte 1 — Unlock Zona Glacial: 1 Couro-de-Magma and 1 Blenio das Bolhas (both from Rachadura Vulcanica).
Parte 2 — Unlock Zenite Abissal: 1 Peixe-Vidro, 1 Predador do Artico, and 1 fish with Criogenico mutation. Criogenia rod boosts the rate significantly.
Parte 3 — Unlock Imminens Virga: 1 Diabo-Marinho Ancestral (Zenite Abissal) and 10 fish with Envenenado delivered.
Parte 4 — Unlock Aguas Calmas: 1 Leviata de Magma, 1 Leviata Congelado, and 1 Anguila Coroada. Keep every copy you find.
Parte 5 — O Fundo (unlock Ponto Zero): 100% of all four sub-zones plus 12000 money spent. Do not arrive at this step broke.

Rod suggestions

  • Ignis — makes the hot branch cleaner.
  • Criogenia — worth crafting before the cold branch drags; directly helps the Criogenico delivery.
  • Zenite — strong stable late-game rod across the whole staircase.
Stage 8 Endgame Branch Closure
Endgame cleanup and consolidation

Main goals

  • Unlock Ponto Zero.
  • Finish Pantano Mushgrove and O Jardim to 100%.
  • Finish the Densa Mata branch.
  • Unlock Templo de Micelio.
  • Close missing crafts, hunts, and mutation projects.

What to do

The Densa Mata branch structure:

  • 50% in both pools starts the line (Prologo)
  • Prologo leads to a poison delivery step
  • Next mission asks for 45 minutes of play time, then checks for 100% in both pools
  • After both pools hit 100%, delivering 3 fish with Arcana unlocks Templo de Micelio

The Arcana mutation is not common. Plan for it as a targeted project rather than a lucky accident.

Key Bottlenecks
Densa Mata Parte 1: 4 fish with Envenenado and 1 Vieira-Lama carrying Envenenado (both from Pantano Mushgrove).
Densa Mata Parte 2: 45 minutes of play time and 100% of both Pantano Mushgrove and O Jardim. The timer runs from when the mission is available, not from session start.
Densa Mata Parte 3 — Unlock Templo de Micelio: 3 fish with the Arcana mutation. Identify which rod produces it and use it intentionally.
Stage 9 Final Core Completion
Late endgame and prestige cleanup

Main goals

  • Finish Ponto Zero.
  • Reach level 20 and unlock Angra Cristal.
  • Complete the remaining main-pool bestiaries.
  • Claim global bestiary rewards.
  • Decide whether to stop at core 100% or continue into hidden content.

What to do

Angra Cristal unlock: level 20, 50% of As profundezas, 5 Salmajoia, and 5 Peixe Tres-Olhos. Both named fish are found in As profundezas. Start banking them the moment you first reach that pool — do not wait until level 20 is close.

Best cleanup order at this stage:

  1. Any pool one or two fish away from 100%.
  2. Any mission chain blocked by one delivery or one mutation.
  3. Any hunt you postponed.
  4. Any craft tied to a rod or branch you still care about.
  5. The last major pools that still need a full push.
Key Bottlenecks
Unlock Angra Cristal: Level 20, 5 Salmajoia, 5 Peixe Tres-Olhos, and 50% of As profundezas.
Escuridao Total... (hunt): Runs in As profundezas. Clear it while working the pool.
Encontro com o Maior (hunt): Runs in Ponto Zero. Clear it as part of your Ponto Zero work.

Suggested Cleanup Order for a Full File

  1. Main-route pools already above 90%.
  2. Mariana sub-zones needed for Ponto Zero.
  3. Pantano Mushgrove and O Jardim if Densa Mata still needs them.
  4. Cave and sky branches that are already in strong progress.
  5. Leftover hunts.
  6. Leftover crafts and rod projects.
  7. Angra Cristal.
  8. Cafeteria and tribute rod content after core completion is secure.

Appraise, Mutations, and Upgrades

Appraise

Use on fish that already matter: mission fish at a bottleneck, rare catches, mutation targets, crafting fish. Do not burn money appraising random inventory.

Mutations — three categories

  • Passive value bonuses — keep and sell. Do not overthink these.
  • Targeted unlock requirements — identify the rod that produces the mutation and use it in the right pool.
  • Rod-exclusive projects — Sereno, Prometido, Solar, Arcana, Criogenico. Each requires the right rod equipped — not just luck. The game gets easier once you stop expecting every important mutation to happen by accident.

Upgrades

  • control — best panic button when catches are becoming too tense.
  • luck — best broad push when a rod is your main progression tool.
  • kg_max — matters when heavy fish are slipping through an otherwise-right rod.

Cafeteria

Cafeteria is not part of the core 25-pool route. The spoiler-light route to find it:

  1. Finish Angra Cristal to 100%.
  2. Claim the bestiary reward.
  3. Pay attention to the special fish that reward gives you.
  4. Use the code tied to that fish in pool selection.

It is excluded from main pool bestiary completion, its fish are excluded from main bestiary completion, and the tribute rods tied to it are excluded from rod bestiary completion. Treat it as bonus completion and curiosity — do not postpone core cleanup because of it.

The Cafe fish from this pool is a crafting ingredient for Retribuição. If you are chasing the tribute rods, hold at least one.

Tribute and Cafeteria Rods

These five rods are excluded from rod bestiary completion. They are the game's answer to "what is left when the run is over?" — recipes that require specific hunts, rare mutations, and in some cases materials only found after reaching the Cafeteria. All five become available only after the Cafeteria is unlocked.

Azul Lâmina
For GotinhaAzul.
Needs: 2 Dragão Azul with Prometido, 2 Dragão Azul with Solar, and 15000 money.

Dragão Azul is the boss of O Chamado Azul hunt in Caverna Luminosa — you need four copies across multiple runs. Solar has a base rate under 0.2%, but the Azul Lâmina itself raises the rate to 3% — a bootstrapping situation that rewards getting one copy first.

Serenidade
For the Fisch devs.
Needs: 2 Peixe-Corda with Sereno (Profundezas Desoladas), 1 Disco de Diamante with Sereno (Aguas Calmas), and 1 Leviata Congelado with Congelado (Zona Glacial).

Sereno only appears with Hollow Dusk or Serenidade equipped. Use Hollow Dusk in Profundezas Desoladas and Aguas Calmas and let it accumulate over time. Congelado is more accessible and appears naturally in cold-zone pools.

Retribuição
As Quatro Formas — the four forms.
Needs: 1 Gladio Escaldante with Carmesim (Rachadura Vulcanica), 1 Peixe Tres-Olhos with Espiral (As profundezas), 1 Lagosta Cristal with Cristalizado (Angra Cristal), 1 Enguia Ancia with Profundo (As profundezas), and 1 Cafe (Cafeteria).

The most geographically demanding recipe — each component comes from a different late zone with a different mutation requirement. The Cafe from the Cafeteria is required.

Sniper
A joke rod. The description acknowledges this.
Needs: 1 Agua-Viva Imperatriz, 20 Pedra with Brilhante, and 10000 money.

Agua-Viva Imperatriz is the boss of Eletricidade Desolada hunt in Profundezas Desoladas. Equip Vara Brilhante in Grande Recife to farm the 20 Pedra — it is a deliberate session, not a lucky accident.

Mãos
The simplest tribute recipe.
Needs: 3 Peixe-Mão from Pantano Mushgrove.

If you finished the swamp for Densa Mata, you likely already have these. The easiest postgame craft by a significant margin.

Final Summary

The best full-completion route in Fisching Overhaul is not the one that rushes the newest unlock. It is the one that keeps the whole file balanced. Push the next branch, keep bestiary progress alive, respect mutation and rod gates, and close pools when they are already nearly done.

When you hit a wall, check the Key Bottlenecks block for that stage. The answer is usually a specific fish in a pool you have already visited, or a mutation from a rod you already own.

If you do that, the run carries you naturally from the opening lagoon to the tribute rods at the end — without collapsing into pure cleanup.
