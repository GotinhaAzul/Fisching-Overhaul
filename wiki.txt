RODS
============================================================

Rods are categorized by their unlock difficulty and playstyle archetype:

ARCHETYPES:
- Skill: Good for catching difficult fish, helps with minigame
- Big Fisher: High Kg Max for catching heavy fish
- Rarity Search: Higher luck for rare fish
- Collectionist: Helps complete bestiary
- Money-Making: Increases gold value
- Overall: Balanced stats for general use
- Joke Rod: Humorous rods with unique effects

UNLOCK DIFFICULTIES:
- Default: Available from start
- Easy: Simple crafting or low requirements
- Medium: Moderate crafting or mission requirements
- Hard: Significant crafting or rare fish requirements
- Very Hard: Very rare requirements or achievements

DISTURBANCE SYSTEM
============================================================
Disturbance is a mechanic that builds up when catching fish in a pool where
a hunt is active. It represents attracting the attention of legendary fish.

How it works:
1. Each catch in a hunt pool adds to disturbance (varies by hunt)
2. Disturbance decays over time during hunts (check_interval)
3. When disturbance reaches the max, legendary fish can spawn
4. Higher disturbance = higher spawn chance
5. If no catch is made for a while, disturbance decays

Spawn Chance Formula:
  base_chance + (disturbance / disturbance_max) * bonus_chance

Example: If base is 5% and disturbance is 50% of max,
  spawn chance = 5% + (0.5 * hunt_rarity_legendary_chance)


APPRAISE SYSTEM
============================================================
Appraising is done at the Market. It allows you to:
1. Reroll fish weight (potentially higher value)
2. Apply a random mutation to the fish

Cost: 35% of the fish's base value (minimum 1 coin)

Appraise Process:
1. Select a fish from inventory
2. Pay the appraise cost
3. Fish weight is rerolled randomly
4. A mutation is applied based on available mutations
5. You can accept or reject the mutation
   - Accept: Keep the mutated fish
   - Reject: Keep original fish (weight still changed)

Note: Each fish can only be appraised once.


ROD UPGRADE SYSTEM
============================================================
Rods can be upgraded at the Market to improve their stats.

Requirements:
- Reach Level 20
- Select a rod to upgrade

How to Upgrade:
1. View upgrade options for a rod
2. A recipe is generated with fish requirements
3. The recipe shows which fish and how many are needed
4. Pay the upgrade cost (25% of rod price, minimum 400 coins)
5. Consume the required fish from inventory
6. Receive a permanent stat bonus

Upgradeable Stats:
- Luck: Increases catch probability
- Kg Max: Increases maximum fish weight
- Control: Improves minigame performance

Bonus Range:
- Minimum: 1% bonus
- Maximum: 25% bonus
- Bonus depends on rarity of fish used

Fish Requirements by Rarity:
  Comum: 4-8 fish
  Incomum: 3-6 fish
  Raro: 2-4 fish
  Epico: 1-3 fish
  Lendario: 1-2 fish
  Mitico: 1 fish

Higher rarity fish = better bonus (up to 25%)


ROD ABILITIES
============================================================
Special abilities that can appear on crafted rods:

SLASH:
  Instantly catches the fish without playing the minigame.
  Chance: Varies by rod (e.g., Sniper: 50%, Trinity: 10%)
  Power: Multiplier for catch strength

SLAM:
  Instantly completes the minigame with a time bonus.
  Chance: Varies by rod (e.g., Vara da Amizade: 65%)
  Time Bonus: Additional time added to minigame

FRENZY:
  Increases catch rate significantly.
  Chance: e.g., Frenesis has 25% frenzy chance

GREED:
  Increases gold value of caught fish.
  Chance: e.g., Midas has 12% greed chance

PIERCE:
  Ignores fish resistance in minigame.
  Chance: e.g., Perforatio has 30% pierce chance

ALTER:
  Alters fish properties (weight, value).
  Only on Zenite rod
  Timecount: Target time value
  Hardcount: Hardness value

RECOVER (not in current rods):
  Automatic retry on failed catches

DUPLICATE (not in current rods):
  Chance for extra copy of fish


BAITS
============================================================
Baits can be used to modify fishing stats. Different baits provide
different bonuses to Luck, Control, and Kg Max.

Baits are typically found in pools or purchased.
Some baits are cheap (lower stats), others are expensive (higher stats).

GAME TIPS
============================================================
1. Start with the default rods (Bambu, Carbono, Fibra, Plastico)
2. Complete missions to unlock new pools
3. Fill bestiary to unlock crafting at level 8
4. Participate in hunts to catch legendary fish
5. Use mutations to increase XP and gold gains
6. Upgrade rods at level 20 for permanent bonuses
7. Appraise fish to get mutations and better weights
8. Higher rarity pools have better fish but are harder
